About Me & My Aspirations

      Hello! I'm Nargis Amiri, and Afghan girl with a passion for learning and empowering others. Growing up, I found comfort an inspiration in books, which have helped me navigate through life's challenges. Despite the obstacles, I've always belive in the power of education and the important of giving back to my community. Whether it's teaching or mentoring, I setive to make a positive impact wherever I can.

      Looking ahead, I'm detemined to continue my education and explore scholarship opportunities to achieve my dreams. My goal is to become a role model for young girls in my community, showing them that wiht determination and hard work, anything is possible.

My Top 3 Role Models:

  1. My mother: My mother has always been my greatest role model. She taught me the value of persverance, kindness, and staying true to my beliefs, even in challenging times.
  2. Malala Yousafzai:As a girl from a region where education was denied, Malala's advocacy for girl's education is powerful.
  3. My teacher Farhadi: My teacher has always encouraged me to pursue my dreams. His belief in my potential has been a huge motivator in helping my continue my eduaction despite challenges.
